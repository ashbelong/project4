I am a 3rd year graphic design student at Kwantlen Polytechnic University. This page is a case study for a project I did in packaging design.

Steadfast

Steadfast Sandwiches is a fictional Sandwich shop in Vancouver, B.C. that uses local ingredients to make their sandwiches. They are advocates for sustainability and strive to use these practices in their restaurants and especially in their packaing.

The Challenge

Create packaging for a range of products while reducing its impact on the environment.

Approach

Steadfast's logo is open and approachable with its script typeface.The overall design relies on its hand-drawn pattern. This pattern sets itself apart from the competition. The hand-drawn aspect gives the brand authenticity.
